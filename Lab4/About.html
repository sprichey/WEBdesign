Many people assume dolphins are fish when they are if fact mammals. Dolphins have warm blood and nurse their young which depending on the breed will stay with them for up to six years.The Orca commonly called the killer whales is in fact not a whale.The orca is the largest of all dolphin species.


There are 49 dolphin and porpoise species that are grouped into six families. These are the dolphin family, the porpoise family, and the river dolphin family. The lifespan for the smaller species is about 20 years while the larger species can live 80 or more years. Dolphins are said to be one of the smartest animals in the world and are capable of solving complex problems. Dolphins are also very social creatures.


The Maui dolphin in New Zeland is critically endangered. As of last year there is said to be only 55 left. They are a subspecies of the Hector's dolphin and the smallest subspecies globally and now one of the rarest.

One of the most excotic species of dolphins is the Amazon River Dolphin also known as the pink dolphin. The pink dolphin is the world's largest fresh water dolphin. This species of dolphin is also critically endangered with the population down by 94% since 2000.There pink color is due to scar tissue although they are born dark grey.
