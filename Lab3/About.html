About Blues Clues

Blues Clues is a show for preschoolers that first aired in 1996 with Steve Burns as Blue's caretaker.



In 2002 Steve Burns left and Donovan Patton "Joe" took over until the show ended in 2006



In 2019 the show was rebooted as Blues Clues and You with Joshua Dela Cruz playing the part of Blue's caretaker.
