Blues Clues and You

Blues Clues and you is a tv show for preschoolers. It teaches them how to be helpful while learning things like shapes, colors, and other base knowledge for children. This show first aired as Blues Clues in 1996 with Steve Burns as Blue's caretaker.

In 2002 Steve left and "Joe" played by Donovan Patton took over until the show ended in 2006. The show has since been revised in 2019 as Blues Clues and You with Josh Dela Cruz.
