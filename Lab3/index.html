Welcome to Blue's Clues

Blue's Clues is a show for preschoolers that first aired in 1996. This is a wonderful show that teaches young children skills such as recognizing feelings and basic preschool knownledge. The show has won several awards and has been on in 120 countries. Blues Clues involves the children as if they were there and motivates them to play along.
